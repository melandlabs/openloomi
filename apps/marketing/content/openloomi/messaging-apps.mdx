# Messaging Apps

Besides using the OpenLoomi interface, you can also interact with OpenLoomi directly through your favorite messaging apps. Once connected, OpenLoomi becomes your AI assistant within those platforms.

> ⚠️ **Important**: All conversations and data are stored **locally** on your device. The app must remain **open** for the message app feature.

---

## Supported Platforms

| Platform    | Status       | Features                       |
| ----------- | ------------ | ------------------------------ |
| Telegram    | 🟢 Available | Commands, chat, reminders      |
| WhatsApp    | 🟢 Available | Chat, reminders, notifications |
| iMessage    | 🟢 Available | Chat, reminders, notifications |
| QQ          | 🟢 Available | Chat, commands, automation     |
| Lark/Feishu | 🟢 Available | Enterprise workflow, commands  |

After connecting your Telegram account, you can also chat with OpenLoomi naturally—just send a message to **Saved Messages** and it will respond.

## iMessage

iMessage integration is **only available on macOS**. Your message data stays on your local device—OpenLoomi only reads recent messages when you use it to generate insights.

- Chat with OpenLoomi naturally
- Set reminders
- Get quick answers from your data
- Manage tasks

## WhatsApp

Connect via QR code or phone pairing, then use OpenLoomi as your personal assistant:

- Set reminders
- Get quick answers
- Manage tasks

## QQ

Connect your QQ account to use OpenLoomi as a chat assistant. Once connected, you can:

- Chat with OpenLoomi naturally
- Use commands for quick actions
- Automate workflows

## Lark/Feishu

Connect Lark/Feishu to enable enterprise workflow automation. Once connected, you can:

- Automate approval workflows
- Handle group messages
- Integrate with enterprise tools
- Use commands for common tasks

---

## Setting Up

1. Go to **Settings** → **Connectors**
2. Click **Connect** on your preferred messaging platform
3. Follow the authorization steps (OAuth, QR code, or device pairing)
4. Start chatting with OpenLoomi!

For detailed setup instructions for each platform, see the [Connectors guide](/docs/openloomi/connectors).
