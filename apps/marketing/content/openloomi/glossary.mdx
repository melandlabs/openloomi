# Glossary

This page defines the core terms used throughout OpenLoomi's documentation. These are the concepts that underpin how the product works.

---

## Connectors

A **Connector** is an integration between OpenLoomi and an external platform (Slack, Telegram, WhatsApp, Gmail, etc.). Connectors are what allow OpenLoomi to read and interact with your messages across different services.

See [Connectors](/docs/openloomi/connectors) for the full list.

---

## Skills

**Skills** are specialized capabilities that OpenLoomi uses to accomplish specific tasks. OpenLoomi has access to skills and automatically selects the right ones based on what you ask. You can also manually select skills in the message composer.

See [Skills](/docs/openloomi/skills) for the full list.

---

## Automation

An **Automation** is a workflow you create to automate repetitive tasks — such as sending scheduled summaries, responding to specific triggers, or routing messages into your OpenLoomi workspace.

See [Automation](/docs/openloomi/automation) to get started.
