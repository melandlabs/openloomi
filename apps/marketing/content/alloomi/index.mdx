# What is Alloomi?

_Understands Your Intent, Orchestrates Execution, Gets Things Done_

Alloomi isn't just another AI assistant—it's a **proactive AI workspace** that **monitors business signals**, **orchestrates tasks autonomously**, and **tracks and validates results end-to-end**. You focus on what matters most; let Alloomi handle the rest.

---

## Core Features

### 📡 Proactive Awareness — Before You Ask

Alloomi continuously monitors signals across platforms—Slack, Email, Calendar, Documents—alerting you to important events proactively instead of waiting for you to check. It surfaces anomalies and critical opportunities before they escalate.

- 🔴 **Urgent**: Investor David just sent a Term Sheet — needs response within 4 hours
- 🟡 **Pending**: Product weekly report incomplete, 3 team members waiting for your review
- 🟢 **Watch**: Competitor launched new feature, comparison analysis report ready

### 🧠 Long-Term Memory — Context Atlas

Alloomi builds persistent knowledge graphs of people, projects, and decisions. Six months later, it still remembers your commitments and context. No more repeating yourself, no more context loss.

- Remembers Q3 partnership direction confirmed with Sarah 3 months ago (budget: $50K)
- Remembers demo feedback 6 weeks ago (needed security compliance materials)
- Today when Sarah emails, automatically pulls historical context and generates reply draft

### 🎯 95% Noise Filtering — Focus on What Matters

Hundreds of daily messages refined into one focused panel. Tells you what you should act on and care about—not drowning you in information overload.

### ⚡ Autonomous Execution — Complete the Loop

Not just telling you what to do—Alloomi **does it**. Drafts replies, schedules meetings, generates reports, tracks and validates results end-to-end.

### 💬 Chat — Assign Tasks Like Talking

Describe your needs in plain language; Alloomi automatically understands intent and breaks it into executable steps. No complex commands to learn. [Learn more →](/docs/alloomi/chat)

> **"Help me prepare investor meeting materials for next Monday"**
>
> Alloomi: "Got it. I've compiled the past 3 months of funding progress, created a competitive analysis, and generated a 10-page PPT framework. Want me to send it for your review?"

### 📱 Messaging Apps — Alloomi in Your Favorite Apps

Use Alloomi directly inside Telegram, WhatsApp, iMessage, QQ, and Lark/Feishu—no separate app required. Assign tasks, get alerts, and receive results right where your conversations already happen. [Learn more →](/docs/alloomi/messaging-apps)

### 🖥️ Desktop Apps — Full Power on Your Computer

Alloomi native desktop apps for **Windows**, **macOS**, and **Linux** give you a distraction-free, always-accessible workspace. Get the full power of Alloomi with keyboard shortcuts, system tray residence, and native notifications—all without opening a browser.

### 🔒 Enterprise-Grade Security — Your Data, Your Sovereignty

Privacy and intelligence aren't mutually exclusive. Alloomi uses **AES-256 end-to-end encryption**, hardware-isolated processing environments without any public gateways, and zero training commitments—your data never trains public AI models. Local-first architecture keeps raw data on your device, while full audit logs and SOC 2 compliance (in progress) give you complete visibility into every data access. [Learn more →](/docs/alloomi/privacy-security)

### 🛠️ 200+ Builtin Skills — Rich Execution Capabilities

From code generation to PDF creation, data analysis to browser automation—Alloomi's Skills ecosystem covers every work scenario and keeps expanding. Import additional skills from any GitHub repo or the Skill Hub to customize your toolkit. [See all Skills →](/docs/alloomi/skills)

## What Makes Alloomi Different?

Most AI assistants are **workflow tools**—you give commands, they execute tasks, with no knowledge of
who you are. Sometimes they surprise you in ways you didn't expect. But usually, most of the time, they
frustrate you, filled with uncertainty, and accompanied by issues of context, memory, cost, and security.

Alloomi is different: it's a **proactive digital partner** that watches, learns, remembers, and acts on
your behalf. The difference is architectural.

When you connect your messaging platforms and integrations to Alloomi, you don't just chat with it through
Telegram, WhatsApp, and other apps—you also sync everything with your permission: raw messages, meetings,
emails, tweets, calendar events, voice calls, and any notes or ideas you've captured. All of this—including
your conversations with Alloomi itself—becomes the **single source of truth** for Alloomi's brain.

Behind the scenes, Alloomi runs a background agent on a continuous sync loop, actively gathering information
from all your connected sources. An agent without this loop can only respond based on stale context. With it,
every conversation—and every moment—makes Alloomi smarter and more aligned with you. When you create a custom
agent role in Alloomi to handle one-off or scheduled tasks, this brain acts as the orchestrator, dramatically
improving execution quality.

### A Complete Intelligence Loop: From Perception to Action

Alloomi implements a full **"Receive → Process → Remember → Understand → Serve"** loop—mirroring how a capable human colleague operates:

| Layer             | What It Does                                                                                                                       |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **📡 Receive**    | Multi-platform, multi-modal ingestion — IM, email, documents, files, web data, voice calls — unified into one pipeline             |
| **⚙️ Process**    | Noise reduction at scale — deduplication, OCR, ASR, intent extraction, semantic clustering — turning chaos into structured signals |
| **🧠 Remember**   | Persistent knowledge graph — people, projects, decisions — survives conversations, platforms, and months of time                   |
| **🎯 Understand** | Deep semantic intent, not just keywords — cross-modal understanding, emotional tone, contextual relevance                          |
| **🚀 Serve**      | Proactive delivery — smart summaries, auto-replies, scheduled reports, personalized alerts — without being asked                   |

---

### Memory That Compounds Over Time

Inside Alloomi, memory is layered into multiple levels of abstraction:

- **Raw information** — original messages, files, transcripts
- **Information insights** — extracted entities, decisions, key events, timline, event execute diff including why, what and how,
- **Contextual memory** — recent conversation state, temporary context
- **Knowledge-base memory** — long-term people/projects/preferences knowledge graph

This layered memory enables Alloomi to reason across both immediate context and deep historical knowledge simultaneously.
A message from a client today is understood in light of a negotiation three months ago—automatically.

Alloomi also exposes clean APIs through skills for all features. Critically, it provides APIs to control its own plane—meaning
Alloomi can invoke itself to complete tasks, enabling self-iteration and continuous improvement without external prompting.

Never miss a critical signal across time zones. Business runs 24/7 globally. Alloomi filters time zone and language noise, capturing high-value opportunities while you sleep—wake up to a refined action list.

### 🧑‍💻 Engineers & Product Teams

Team memory that never decays. Transform discussions scattered across Slack, Jira, and documents into structured knowledge. Auto-generate weekly reports, sync missed context, eliminate "context rot."

### 🚀 Founders & Salespeople

One person does the work of many, at scale. Alloomi learns your communication style, automatically maintains hundreds of client relationships, follows up on leads, generates personalized proposals—never burns out.

Stop wasting hours on manual dev reports and issue triaging. Every night at 10 PM, Alloomi automatically pulls commits, compiles a structured dev report, emails the team, fetches new GitHub issues, rewrites them with full context, and syncs everything to Linear. Run your X (Twitter) account on complete autopilot. Every 4 hours, Alloomi researches trending topics, engages with relevant accounts, posts approved content, and tracks every action—while you simply approve before anything goes live.
