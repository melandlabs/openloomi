# Settings

Access All settings from the **sidebar → ⚙️ Settings** (or click your profile avatar).

Settings are organized into five sections:

- [General](#general)
- [Personalization](#personalization)
- [Billing](#billing)
- [Storage](#storage)
- [About](#about)

---

## General

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/alloomi/setting-general.png" />
</div>

Controls how Alloomi looks and behaves at a system level.

**Language** — changes the UI language. Supported: English, 简体中文.

**AI Soul** — defines the personality and tone Alloomi uses when summarizing, replying, and surfacing insights. Choose from five presets or write a custom prompt:

| Preset         | Best for                                                      |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Default**    | General use — balanced and neutral                            |
| **Strategist** | Big-picture thinkers who want high-level synthesis            |
| **Executor**   | Action-oriented operators who want direct, zero-fluff output  |
| **Connector**  | Relationship-focused users who care about people and networks |
| **Calm**       | Users who prefer a grounded, low-noise experience             |
| **Custom**     | Write your own prompt (max 5,000 characters)                  |

> **Tip:** If you're just getting started, pick a preset that matches how you think. Switch to Custom once you have a sense of what's working.

**Refresh Interval** — how often Alloomi syncs new data from your connectors. Options: 5 / 15 / 30 / 60 / 120 minutes. More frequent = more up-to-date, but uses more credits.

## Personalization

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/alloomi/setting-personalization.png" />
</div>

Personalization has four sub-sections — work through them to sharpen Alloomi's understanding of you.

### Avatar

Upload a profile picture (PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF — up to 5 MB). This appears next to your name across Alloomi.

### Basic

Identical to the [AI Soul](#general) settings above — language, AI Soul preset, and Refresh Interval are also accessible here.

### Interests

Tells Alloomi which people and topics matter most, so it can push them to the top of your Focus view.

Two sections:

- **People I Follow** — enter names, handles, or nicknames. Alloomi will watch for their activity across connected platforms.
  - _e.g., Jess from Finance, @founders channel_
- **Topics I Follow** — enter keywords, project names, or short descriptions. Alloomi uses these as signals when ranking events.
  - _e.g., product roadmap, Series A, customer churn_

**Quick-start templates** — click any card to add it instantly:

| Template                 | Example                                                |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Track important people   | Watch Zhang's updates about the project contract       |
| Track project progress   | Track major milestones for the Alloomi project         |
| Capture pending actions  | Capture action items from the product discussion group |
| Follow trending topics   | Follow updates about major AI product releases         |
| Monitor tools & systems  | Monitor subscription renewal alerts from your tools    |
| Collect team assignments | Collect tasks assigned by the team                     |
| Consolidate feedback     | Summarize feedback about the new feature               |
| Capture achievements     | Capture product launch highlights and user milestones  |

> **Tip:** Interests directly influence how events are ranked as **High / Medium / Low** on your Focus page.

### Role

Tells Alloomi about you so its summaries and prioritization reflect your actual work context.

Three fields:

- **My Industry** — select up to 4 (e.g., IT/Tech, Finance, E-commerce, Healthcare…)
- **My Role** — up to 4 roles (e.g., Founder, Product Lead, Engineer, BD)
- **About Me** — a freeform description of your work, priorities, and what you spend your time on (up to 5,000 characters)

> _e.g., I'm a co-founder of a B2B SaaS startup, leading product and fundraising. I spend most of my day in Slack and email, and care most about customer renewals and our next funding round._

Keep it honest and specific — write it like you're briefing a new teammate on day one, not writing a resume bio.

## Billing

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/alloomi/setting-billing.png" />
</div>

Manage your subscription and usage.

**Your Plan** — shows your current plan (Free, Pro, Team) with upgrade or manage options.

**Usage** — tracks your consumption of Alloomi credits across:

- Search & Synthesis
- AI Soul (Auto Track)
- AI Soul (Chat)

**Credits** — shows current balance. Click **Top Up** to purchase more credits.

## Storage

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/alloomi/setting-storage.png" />
</div>

Manages local storage used by Alloomi.

**Storage Breakdown** — visual chart showing space used by:

- Sessions
- Events
- Vector store
- Skills
- Agent browser
- Logs
- Cache

**Actions:**

- **Clear Vector Store Data** — removes stored vector embeddings
- **Clear Logs** — removes log files

## About

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/alloomi/setting-about.png" />

**Version** — shows your current Alloomi version. Click **Check for updates** to fetch the latest release.

**Give Feedback** — opens a feedback form to send suggestions or report bugs directly to the team.

## Language Settings

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/alloomi/setting-language.png" />
</div>

Changes the UI language separately from the output language.

Open: **⚙️ Settings → General → Language** (or click your profile avatar → **Language Settings**)

Supported: **English** and **简体中文 (Simplified Chinese)**
