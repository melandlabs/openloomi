# Events

**The Focus view is where Alloomi tells you what actually needs your attention.**

Every message from every connected platform gets analyzed. Alloomi identifies what matters, groups related messages into a single **Event**, and surfaces them by priority — so instead of scanning hundreds of unread messages, you see a clear, ranked list of real things happening.

## How Events Are Prioritized

Alloomi automatically assigns each event a priority level based on content, urgency signals, and your Interests settings. Events are grouped into three bands:

| Priority   | What it means                                                                                   |
| ---------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **High**   | Needs your attention now — urgent asks, escalations, or activity from people you follow closely |
| **Medium** | Worth reviewing today — active threads, updates on things you care about                        |
| **Low**    | Background noise — informational, low-signal, or things that can wait                           |

You can **drag any event between priority groups** to override Alloomi's judgment. Your manual moves are remembered and help Alloomi calibrate over time.

## Bulk Actions

When you have multiple events to handle at once, select them with the checkbox and act in bulk:

- **Unpin** — only move handled events out of your main focus view.
- **Archive** — move handled events out of your focus and context views.

## Inside an Event

Click any event card to open its detail view. Everything about that event lives here.

### Read the Full Context

See the AI-generated description, all related messages, everyone involved, and a timeline of how the event evolved — without opening five different apps.

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/alloomi/event-detail.png" />
</div>

The **Update Record** timeline shows key milestones: when the event was created, when things changed, when it was resolved. You see the shape of a situation at a glance.

Switch to the **Messages tab** to see the raw thread. Each message shows its source — exactly which platform, channel, and person it came from, and when.

</div>

### Ask Alloomi About the Event

Click the **@** button at the bottom of the event to open a chat with Alloomi about that specific thread. Alloomi reads the full conversation and answers based on what was actually said.

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/alloomi/event-conversation.png" />
</div>

Useful things to ask:

- **"What is this about?"** — instant summary without reading the whole thread
- **"Who are the key people?"** — understand the players fast
- **"What decisions were made?"** — surface conclusions buried in a long thread
- **"What should I reply?"** — get a draft response based on context

### Reply Without Leaving Alloomi

See a message that needs a response? Switch to the **Reply tab** inside the event. You can compose and send a reply directly back to the original platform — no need to switch apps.

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/alloomi/event-source-reply.png" />
</div>

Alloomi offers three reply suggestions to get you started:

| Suggestion                     | When to use it                                         |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Ask** — Ask Clarification    | You need more information before acting                |
| **Act** — Acknowledge Request  | You're confirming you've received and will handle it   |
| **Alter** — Suggest Refinement | You want to push back or propose a different direction |

Choose one as a starting point, edit it, and send — or write from scratch.

**Two tools to polish your reply:**

- **AI Translation** — Write in your language, send in theirs. Supports English and Chinese, with more coming.
- **AI Polishing** — Paste a rough message and Alloomi cleans it up: fixes grammar, adjusts tone (formal / casual / friendly), makes it clearer.
